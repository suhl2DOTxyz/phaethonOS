## Wallpaper Concept 1: "New Eridu Gridlock" (Default)
- **Aesthetic**: Urban Cyberpunk / Street Art. High contrast, grit-lined, and graphic.
- **Composition**:
  - **Foreground**: A sharp, flat-shaded side profile of a skater character looking out over a metropolitan concrete landscape. Stylized ink contours, heavy black outlines, and comic-style cross-hatching for shade.
  - **Background**: Stylized street signs, towering concrete facades, and concrete pipes under a starless dark sky. Large geometric road slashes overlaying the background elements.
  - **Color Palette**:
    - Backdrop: Pure Near-Black (`#0a0a0a`)
    - Accents, graffiti, and highlights: Neon Lime-Yellow (`#C8FF00`)
    - Graphic details: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)
    - Muted structures: Deep Gray (`#444444` to `#888888`)
  - **Branding**: The golden Phaethon compass star emblem (`#D4AF37`) rendered as a clean, flat sticker or stencil on a foreground concrete pillar.
